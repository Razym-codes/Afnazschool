Today I’m gonna talk about a technology that’s gonna reshape our world, called Blockchain⛓️

Imagine I have a coin and you don’t have a coin. When I give my coin to you, you have one coin and I have zero coins.

With digital coins this is different. If I have a digital coin and copy mine to your computer, I still have a copy of the coin on my computer. I have to promise to erase my coin when I give you my coin, otherwise that would be cheating.

To make sure other people don’t think we’re cheating, we’re keeping the score on a big schoolboard with a permanent maker. This marker can never be erased once it has dried, which means nobody can ever change the score. This way everybody can see I gave a digital coin to you and didn’t keep mine too. If I ever tried to give my coin to someone else, people could look on the board and see I was cheating.

Because everybody can come and take a look at the board, nobody has to believe our pinky promise that we didn’t secretly keep a copy of our digital coin.

We also don’t need the teacher to check all the time of nobody is cheating.

Blockchain is basically a network of computers called nodes which all have same history of transactions. So instead of one company or a database which holds all the information, now the information is spread across whole of the network.
